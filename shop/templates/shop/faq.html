Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Orders" section. You’ll find real-time updates about your order status there.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods, including MPESA, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. More options will be added soon.

3. Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, you can return or exchange a product within 7 days of delivery. The product must be in its original condition. Visit our Return Policy page for more details.

4. What should I do if I face issues during payment?

If you encounter issues during payment, check if your payment details are correct or if there’s an issue with your payment method. If the problem persists, contact our Customer Support.

5. How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days, depending on your location. You will receive updates via email or SMS once your order is shipped.

6. How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team through the Contact Us page, or email us at brianfadi20@gmail.com. You can also call us at +254 741138292.
